# $VEXOR Staking — Coming Soon

The on-chain $VEXOR RevShare staking pool is **not yet live**. The $VEXOR token contract has not been deployed on Base, and the staking pool will go live alongside the token launch.

This guide will be republished with the complete mechanics — how it works, the math, worked examples, contract addresses, and risks — once the contracts ship.

## What will the pool do at launch?

Single-sided $VEXOR staking on Base. No lock, no tier — withdraw anytime. The protocol will push $VEXOR rewards into the pool periodically; each staker's claimable balance updates pro-rata to their share of `totalStaked`.

Rewards will be funded from protocol revenue (chat / task fees). The exact economics will be finalized before launch and published here.

Follow [@vexorterminal](https://x.com/vexorterminal) on X or the [Watchtower](https://t.me/VexorAeonWatchtowerbot) Telegram bot. The launch announcement will include:

Until those addresses are published here, there is **no live $VEXOR contract** — anything marketed as $VEXOR today is not associated with Vexor Terminal.
